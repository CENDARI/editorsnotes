About

CENDARI is an acronym for the Collaborative European Digital Archive Infrastructure, a 4-year, European Commission-funded project, which aims to integrate digital archives and resources for medieval and World War One history.

The aim of CENDARI is to produce a Virtual Research Environment (or VRE) which will allow scholars to access historical resources across institutional and national boundaries.

The core of the VRE is the Note Taking Environment, along with the Archival Directory and Archival Research Guides, and an advanced search functionality that includes standard, faceted and semantic search. Upon completion of the project, the Note Taking Environment, the Archival Directory and Archive Research Guides will be integrated in the Virtual Research Environment.
